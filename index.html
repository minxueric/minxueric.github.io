Research Scientist at IBM Research China








I am a research scientist at IBM Research China since July 2019. Before I joined IBM, I was a Ph.D. student at Department of Computer and Science in Tsinghua University from September 2014 to June 2019. My supervisor at Tsinghua is Prof. Ting Chen. During my Ph.D. career, I also spent one year as a jointly Ph.D. student in Cornell University supervised by Prof. Fei Wang, funded by China Scholarship Council.

Currently, I focus on applying artificial intelligence (AI) or machine learning methods to healthcare data analysis. In particular, I am interested in data representation for Electronic Health Records (EHRs), time series clinical data, and medical images, using either deep learning or statistical models. I am committed to developing models with robustness, interpretability, safety and trustability for various healthcare scenarios.
